About Shri Kainchi Dham

A Spiritual Haven in the Hills of Kumaon

Kainchi Dham is a beautiful secluded mountain ashram located in the Kumaon Hills in Uttarakhand. Set amidst lush greenery, the ashram is dedicated to Shri Neem Karoli Baba (Maharaj-ji), a highly revered Hindu saint known for his unconditional love, miracles, and teachings of selfless service.

The temple acts as a sacred space for meditation and spiritual awakening, attracting devotees from all around the globe—including visionaries like Steve Jobs and Mark Zuckerberg—who come here to experience the profound peace and high spiritual vibrations of the valley.
